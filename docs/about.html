About

I am an Assistant Professor of Mathematics at the National Institute of Technology Warangal. My research lies at the intersection of Scientific Machine Learning, Dynamical Systems, and Control Theory.

I develop mathematically rigorous learning and computational methods for problems arising in differential equations, dynamical systems, scientific computing, and control.

My research combines mathematical analysis, numerical methods, optimization, and machine learning. A central theme of my work is the integration of modern artificial intelligence with classical mathematical theory to develop reliable, interpretable, and theoretically grounded computational methods.

Prior to joining NIT Warangal, I was a Postdoctoral Fellow at the Laboratoire de Mécanique Paris-Saclay, École Normale Supérieure Paris-Saclay, Université Paris-Saclay, France, where I worked under the supervision of Prof. Ludovic Chamoin. My postdoctoral research focused on learning-based Model Predictive Control, neural networks, and Scientific Machine Learning.
